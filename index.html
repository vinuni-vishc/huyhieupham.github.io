Hieu Pham

I am currently an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at VinUniversity, where I work on Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence (AI), and Smart Health Applications. I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science at the Toulouse Computer Science Research Institute (IRIT) at The University of Toulouse, France, in 2019. In 2023, I became a Visiting Scholar at the Coordinated Science Laboratory at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), USA. From 2022, I has been serving as the Associate Director of the VinUni-Illinois Smart Health Center (VISHC), that aims to conduct high-impact research on biomedical AI to provide widely accessible health monitoring and improvement for people all over the world.

I spent two years as a Research Scientist at Vingroup Big Data Institute, conducting research on Medical Imaging Analysis. In 2015, I spent half a year at the ICA laboratory for working on a robotized airplane inspection project, called Air-Cobot, lead by AKKA Technologies and AIRBUS group.

Before that, I did my bachelor's at the Hanoi University of Science and Technology (HUST). My graduate internship was done at International Research Institute MICA, Hanoi, Vietnam and AGIM laboratory, Universit√© Grenoble Alpes, Grenoble, France.

Research Interests

My research interests include Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Computer Vision, especially their applications in Smart Healthcare, e.g. Medical Imaging Diagnosis, AI-based Computer-aided Diagnosis (AI-CAD), AI-assisted Diagnosis and Treatment, AI-assisted Disease Prevention and Risk Monitoring. Check my latest publications here.

I am looking for self-motivated Ph.D., Research Assistants and Interns, who are interested in artificial intelligence for smart healthcare, computer vision, machine/deep learning, and natural language processing. If you are interested in doing research with me, please send me an email (hieu.ph@vinuni.edu.vn) with your CV and transcripts. If you are a VinUni student and interested in conducting research, you can reach me via email or meet me in-person at VISHC Office, G Building, VinUni Campus for further discussion. Remote collaboration is also welcome!
